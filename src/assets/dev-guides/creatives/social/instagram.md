# Platform Guide: Instagram

> [!NOTE]
> Instagram is a visual-first platform where high-quality aesthetics and information density (carousels) are key.

## Aspect Ratios

- **Circle (Profile)**: 1:1 (Ideal: 1080x1080).
- **Square (Feed)**: 1:1 (1080x1080).
- **Portrait (Feed Focus)**: 4:5 (1080x1350).
- **Stories/Reels**: 9:16 (1080x1920).

## Visual Strategy

### 1. The PFP Circle

Always center your logo or subject. Most GenAI will produce squares; ensure the critical content fits within a circle that covers 70% of the square's center.

### 2. Carousel Architecture

- **Cover**: High-contrast, vibrant colors. Use "The Hook" prompt strategy.
- **Inner Slides**: Consistent background style. Use **Glassmorphism** or **Minimalist** presets to avoid cluttering text.
- **CTA Slide**: High-visibility button mockup.

### 3. Story Safe Zones

Keep text away from:

- **Top 250px**: Account info and "Close" button.
- **Bottom 250px**: Interaction bar (Send Message/Share).
- **Sides 50px**: Accidental tap area for navigation.
