# Platform Guide: LinkedIn

> [!NOTE]
> LinkedIn requires professional authority, high readability, and clean visual layouts.

## Aspect Ratios

- **Profile Picture**: 1:1 (400x400 Recommended).
- **Banner (Personal)**: 1584x396 (4:1).
- **Post (Square)**: 1:1 (1080x1080).
- **Post (Landscape)**: 1.91:1 (1200x627).

## Visual Strategy

### 1. Banner Hierarchy

The LinkedIn banner is ultra-wide.

- **Center-Right**: Focus on the value proposition text.
- **Left Side**: Avoid critical info, as the profile picture overlays this area on mobile.
- **Vibe**: Use **Corporate Bauhaus** or **Cyber-professional** styles.

### 2. Post Legibility

- Use high-contrast backgrounds (Dark or Light Minimalist).
- Avoid script/handwritten fonts for key info.
- Focus on "Data-Rich" visuals: simplified charts or step-down layouts.
