# Platform Guide: TikTok

> [!NOTE]
> TikTok is the platform of movement and rapid visual patterns. Safe zones are extremely tight.

## Aspect Ratios

- **Video**: 9:16 (1080x1920).
- **Profile Pic**: 1:1 (Minimalist, high-focus).

## Visual Strategy

### 1. Vertical Safe Zones (The "L" Pattern)

TikTok's UI overlays cover a lot of the screen:

- **Right Side**: Like, Comment, Share buttons.
- **Bottom**: Captions, Song title, Hashtags.
- **Top**: Search icon and "For You" tabs.
- **Rule**: Keep critical visuals and text in the **Left-Center** area (Avoid the bottom 25% and the right 15%).

### 2. High-Pace Visuals

- Use **Neobrutalist** or **Vaporwave** styles for high-contrast visibility.
- Transition-friendly backgrounds: Use consistent lighting across assets to allow smooth cuts.
