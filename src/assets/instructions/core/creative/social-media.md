# Creative Hub — Social Media Standards

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Use these technical specs to ensure assets are optimized for each platform's safe zones and aspect ratios.

## Multi-Platform Specs

| Platform      | Type              | Aspect Ratio  | Resolution (px)       | Safe Zones / Notes                                         |
| :------------ | :---------------- | :------------ | :-------------------- | :--------------------------------------------------------- |
| **All**       | Profile Pic (PFP) | 1:1           | 1080x1080             | **CROP CIRCLE**: Keep subject central (Safe: central 70%). |
| **Instagram** | Post              | 1:1 or 4:5    | 1080x1080 / 1080x1350 | Center elements for 1:1 crop in profile grid.              |
| **Instagram** | Story / Reel      | 9:16          | 1080x1920             | Avoid top 250px (profile) and bottom 250px (UI/text).      |
| **TikTok**    | Video             | 9:16          | 1080x1920             | Avoid right side (buttons) and bottom (captions).          |
| **LinkedIn**  | Post              | 1.91:1 or 1:1 | 1200x627 / 1080x1080  | Professional/Clean. High-contrast typography.              |
| **LinkedIn**  | Cover             | 4:1           | 1584x396              | Profile picture overlays on the left/center on mobile.     |
| **YouTube**   | Banner            | 16:9          | 2560x1440             | **Safe Area (Mobile/Web): 1546x423px (Center).**           |
| **YouTube**   | Thumbnail         | 16:9          | 1280x720              | High-impact text, emotive subjects.                        |

## Content Strategy Rules

### 1. The Carousels Rule (Instagram/LinkedIn)

- **Slide 1 (The Hook)**: Aggressive value proposition + visual anchor.
- **Slide 2-N (The Meat)**: Bullet points, diagrams, or "Step-by-Step".
- **Last Slide (Call to Action)**: Explicit instruction (Comment, Link, Share).

### 2. Safe Area Guardrails

When generating 9:16 content (Stories/TikTok):

- Keep all critical text/logos within the **Center 70%** of the height.
- Leave the top and bottom edges for platform-specific overlays (usernames, hashtags, music).

## Social Media Logic Checklist

- [ ] Is the primary text readable in a small thumbnail?
- [ ] Are safe zones respected for UI overlays?
- [ ] Is the "Hook" visible in the first 0.5 seconds of scrolling?
